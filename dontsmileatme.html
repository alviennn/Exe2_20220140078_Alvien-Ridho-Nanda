Don't Smile at Me

Description:

Don't Smile at Me is the debut extended play by American singer-songwriter Billie Eilish. It was released on August 11, 2017, through Darkroom and Interscope Records. The EP was re-released on August 10, 2018. The EP includes the single "Ocean Eyes," which was released in November 2016.
